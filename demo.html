Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g cator sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
Self-raising flour
Apre-mixed combination of flour and leakving agent(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. preheat the oven to 90 C.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip togehter the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. fold in flour.
  5. poured mixture into prepared pan.
  6. bake for 20 minutes in preheated oven.
  7. cool in the pan over a wire rack.
